## The Problem We Solve

The web app ecosystem faces a fundamental trust gap. Today, billions of users rely on centralized app stores from Apple and Google to vet the software they install. Progressive Web Apps — lightweight, cross-platform, and offline-capable — represent the future of software distribution, yet they have no equivalent trust infrastructure. Users have no way to verify whether a PWA is safe before installing it. Developers have no streamlined path to publish, verify, and distribute their PWAs to a broad audience.

SafeLaunch closes that gap.

## What SafeLaunch Is

SafeLaunch is a curated marketplace where developers publish Progressive Web Apps and users discover, evaluate, and install them with confidence. Every app submitted to SafeLaunch passes through our **6-layer AI-powered vetting pipeline** before it reaches a single user:

1. **Static Intake** — Secrets detection, malware signatures, and binary blob scanning
2. **Sandboxed Build & SBOM** — Isolated Docker builds with full dependency inventory and CVE scanning
3. **Post-Build Artifact Analysis** — Detection of hidden iframes, crypto-miners, redirect chains, and obfuscated code
4. **Dynamic Sandbox Analysis** — Headless browser execution with real-time network monitoring and fingerprint detection
5. **Policy & Compliance** — Privacy policy verification, terms of service, and category-specific rule enforcement
6. **Reputation & History** — Publisher track record, version history analysis, and abuse report monitoring

Apps that pass receive a public AI-Verified trust badge and a transparent safety report visible to every user. Apps that fail receive clear, explainable rejection reasons — no mystery takedowns.
